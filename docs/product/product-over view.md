### 1,Product Overview

Product Name: SmartAttend – Student Attendance System

  SmartAttend is a mobile application designed to simplify the process of recording and managing classroom attendance. Instead of manually calling student names, teachers scan QR codes using their mobile device to instantly record attendance.

 The system reduces errors, saves time, and allows teachers to easily review attendance records for any course or date.

## 4, Product Workflow

1.Teacher logs into the application.

2.Teacher selects the course.

3.Teacher starts an attendance session.

4.Students show their QR code.

5.Teacher scans the QR codes.

6.Attendance is recorded automatically.

7.Teacher can review attendance later.
